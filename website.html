Introducing AeroMouse: An intuitive, driverless 3D mouse that translates natural hand gestures into standard 2D computer controls.

Watch Demo View Code

The Mouse Has Been Chained to the Desk for 40 Years.

We're physically tied to a flat surface, which is restrictive, un-ergonomic, and clunky.

It's Restrictive

Using a mouse is awkward on a couch, in bed, or on a small airplane tray. We are physically tethered to a flat surface.

It's Un-ergonomic

Prolonged, repetitive use of a traditional mouse can lead to wrist strain and discomfort (RSI).

It's Clunky

Presenters are stuck behind a podium just to click "next" or must carry a separate, limited "clicker" device.

The Solution

Control Your Cursor From the Air.

Natural Gesture Mapping

AeroMouse translates natural 3D hand motions into standard 2D mouse commands. Tilt your hand to move the cursor. Twist your wrist to scroll. It feels natural because it is.

Driverless & Universal

The magic is in the hack. By emulating a standard Bluetooth HID (Human Interface Device), AeroMouse works out-of-the-box with *any* computer or tablet. No drivers, no setup. It just works.

The Tech Behind the Magic

Hardware

  • MCU: ESP32 (for Bluetooth)
  • Sensor: MPU-6050 6-Axis Gyro
  • 3D Printed Enclosure
  • LiPo Battery & Charger

Firmware (The "Brain")

  • Written in C++ (Arduino)
  • Sensor Fusion (Kalman Filter)
  • Gyro Drift Compensation
  • Custom Gesture Mapping Logic

Software (The "Hack")

  • Bluetooth HID Profile
  • Emulates Standard Mouse
  • Sends Cursor & Scroll Events
  • Result: 100% Driverless!
